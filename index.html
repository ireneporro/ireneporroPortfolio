About

Designing clarity for complex products.

I am Irene Porro, a Product Designer based in Europe and relocating to Prague. I have around five years of experience designing B2B SaaS, platform products, dashboards, CRM systems and data-heavy interfaces.

My strongest area is UI craft: visual clarity, hierarchy, consistency, scalable components, clean Figma files, interaction states and dev-friendly execution.

UX supports that craft through research, flows, IA and product reasoning, so interfaces are not only polished but useful, coherent and easy to understand.

Interface QA

Before a screen feels finished, I check the things users and engineers will actually touch.

  • Hierarchy is visible without explanation.
  • Empty, loading and error states are designed.
  • Components behave consistently across flows.
  • Handoff notes remove implementation guesswork.
Pattern check

Repeated flows should reuse decisions, not reinvent them screen by screen.

Handoff note

States define quality.

Hover, focus, disabled, validation and edge cases are part of the product, not polish at the end.

Design with purpose

Good UI makes the next decision easier for users, product teams and developers.
